Instructions: best results on a desktop or laptop
Use arrow keys to move player/cat around and collect "fireflies"
Use the space bar to use "magic" on the enemies
The game will end when all enemies are gone and all fireflies are collected
Mobile device:
Can move cat around my touching different places on screen to collect "fireflies"
(Plan to add a virtual joystick at a later time for mobile)
To start a new game, best to refresh page, but can just click title screen once again
